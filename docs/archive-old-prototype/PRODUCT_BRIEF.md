# RideTune Product Brief

## Working Name

The app is called **RideTune**.

## Core Promise

Portuguese:

> Afina a suspensão da tua mota ao teu peso, bagagem e passageiro — sem andar às cegas.

English:

> Set your motorcycle suspension for your weight, luggage and passenger — without guessing.

## Product Positioning

RideTune is a mobile suspension setup assistant for motorcyclists. It helps riders find responsible starting points for suspension adjustment based on motorcycle model, rider weight, equipment, passenger, luggage, riding scenario, road surface and preferred feel.

It is especially focused on adventure, trail, touring and sport-touring motorcycles, where load changes dramatically between solo riding, travel, passenger use, top cases and side cases.

The app must never claim perfect setup accuracy. It should present every result as a recommended starting point and encourage the rider to confirm with the official manual, measure sag and test safely.

## Target User

The target user is a normal rider who wants the motorcycle to feel safer, more comfortable and more predictable without needing to become a suspension specialist.

The tone should be:

- technical, but simple;
- responsible, but confident;
- visual, but not noisy;
- premium, but accessible;
- focused on safety and practical usefulness.

## Product Philosophy

The app should explain suspension adjustments in practical language:

- what to adjust;
- in which direction;
- how many clicks, turns or steps only when the data is confirmed;
- when to keep the standard value;
- when to measure sag again;
- when to consult a technician.

Preferred wording:

- recommended starting point;
- suggested adjustment;
- confirm with sag measurement;
- always test safely;
- check the official owner manual;
- do not exceed motorcycle load limits;
- make one change at a time.

Avoid:

- promises of perfect setup;
- invented exact values;
- overly complex suspension jargon;
- hiding safety warnings behind small print.

## Main User Outcomes

A user should be able to:

1. Choose motorcycle brand, model, year and version.
2. Enter rider weight, riding gear weight, passenger weight and luggage weight.
3. Choose riding use, surface and preferred setup feel.
4. Receive a clear suspension setup recommendation.
5. Learn how to measure sag.
6. Diagnose common ride symptoms.
7. Save, compare and reuse setups.

## Main App Sections

### Onboarding

Three simple slides:

1. Choose your motorcycle.
2. Enter weight and load.
3. Get a recommended starting setup.

### Home

Show:

- current motorcycle;
- active setup;
- current load;
- Get Setup;
- Sag Guide;
- Fix My Ride;
- My Setups.

### My Bike

Allow selection of:

- brand;
- model;
- year;
- version;
- suspension type;
- available adjustments.

Show clear warnings when OEM data is not confirmed.

### Load Setup

Inputs:

- rider weight;
- gear weight;
- passenger weight;
- side case weight;
- top case weight;
- extra bag weight.

Show total load visually and classify it as light, medium, heavy or very heavy.

### Riding Mode

Options:

- Comfort;
- Balanced;
- Sport;
- Touring;
- Off-road Light;
- Heavy Load.

### Result

Show:

- setup scenario;
- objective;
- front recommendation;
- rear recommendation;
- preload;
- rebound;
- compression;
- notes;
- safety warnings;
- save setup;
- open Sag Guide;
- open Fix My Ride;
- compare with previous setup.

### Sag Guide

A practical assistant to measure static sag and rider sag.

### Fix My Ride

A symptom-based diagnostic helper.

### My Setups

Allow users to:

- view;
- edit;
- duplicate;
- compare;
- delete;
- export setups.

### Settings

Preferences:

- language;
- units;
- theme;
- main motorcycle;
- safety disclaimer;
- data export;
- free/Pro plan.

## Visual Direction

The app should feel like a premium digital motorcycle dashboard:

- dark mode by default;
- futuristic, clean and technical;
- electric blue primary color;
- neon green or technical orange secondary color;
- yellow/orange warnings;
- discreet red danger states;
- white primary text;
- light grey secondary text;
- large buttons;
- clear icons;
- smooth transitions;
- visual load indicators;
- short, clear text.

## Monetization Direction

Freemium model.

Free:

- one saved motorcycle;
- basic solo setup;
- basic sag guide;
- limited setup history;
- selected free models;
- limited Fix My Ride access.

Pro:

- multiple motorcycles;
- unlimited setups;
- passenger and luggage scenarios;
- complete Fix My Ride;
- setup export;
- full history;
- adjuster photos;
- premium model packs;
- electronic suspension support;
- cloud backups.

Suggested pricing:

- monthly: 2.99 EUR to 4.99 EUR;
- annual: 19.99 EUR to 29.99 EUR;
- early lifetime: 19.99 EUR to 39.99 EUR.

## Safety Disclaimer

The app must repeatedly communicate:

This application provides recommended starting points only. Always confirm values in the official motorcycle manual. Make small changes, one at a time. Test in a safe place. Do not exceed motorcycle load limits. Check tyre pressure. If unsure, consult a qualified mechanic or suspension technician.
