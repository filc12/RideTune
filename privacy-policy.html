RideTune

Privacy Policy

1. Overview

RideTune ("the App") is a motorcycle suspension setup assistant. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

2. Data We Collect

RideTune does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — including rider weight, passenger weight, luggage weight, and motorcycle selection — is stored locally on your device only.

  • No account registration is required.
  • No data is sent to external servers.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No advertising networks are used.

3. Local Storage

The App stores your preferences and saved setups locally on your device using standard mobile storage APIs. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the App.

4. Third-Party Services

RideTune does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect personal data.

5. Children's Privacy

RideTune is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ridetuneapp@gmail.com.
