## EN English (default)

**Title:** RideTune — Motorcycle Setup

**Short description:** Dial in your motorcycle suspension with OEM-based setup data.

**Long description:**
RideTune helps motorcycle riders find a better suspension setup based on their bike, rider weight, luggage and passenger load.

Select your motorcycle, enter your load, and get a practical starting point for preload, rebound and compression adjustments — using OEM-based, verified, calculated or estimated setup data.

RideTune helps you understand:
- Front and rear preload, rebound and compression
- How to count suspension clicks correctly
- Whether each setup is OEM, verified, calculated or estimated
- Recommended sag targets for your load
- Basic suspension diagnostics

The free plan includes 1 bike, solo mode, a basic sag guide and diagnostics.
Premium unlocks all bikes, all load modes, saved setups and 5 languages: English, Portuguese, Spanish, French and German.

RideTune supports adventure, naked, sport, sport touring, supermoto and scrambler motorcycles from brands such as KTM, Yamaha, Honda, Suzuki, CFMoto, Kove and more.

Start with a better baseline. Adjust with more confidence.
